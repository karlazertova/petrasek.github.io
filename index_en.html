About Filip

Ing. Filip Petrásek, Ph.D., is a theoretical physicist who graduated from the Faculty of Nuclear Sciences and Physical Engineering at CTU in Prague. His focus is on mathematical physics, specifically string theory. Currently, he lectures at this faculty, teaching Thermodynamics and Quantum Physics, and leads the Physics Seminar. His research focuses on new theoretical approaches in string theory and fundamental structures of matter.

Biography

Filip was born in 1990. After graduating from high school in Polička, he pursued his studies in physics at CTU in Prague. In 2012, he received his Ph.D. in mathematical physics. Since then, he has been working as a researcher and educator at the Faculty of Nuclear Sciences and Physical Engineering in Prague.

Publications

  • String Theory and Its Applications - ČVUT Press, 2014
  • New Approaches to Particle Dynamics - Journal of Mathematical Physics, 2018
  • Stun in Quantum Gravity - Physics Review D, 2020
